Pentru cei ce nu vor sa foloseasca ATmelStudio:
1) Aflare cai executabile nevoite(avr-gcc,avrasm2,avr-obj,avr-dude).
    ATmelStudio>Tools>Options>Package configuration
    ex:E:\atmelstudio\7.0\toolchain\avr8\avr8-gnu-toolchain\bin\avr-gcc.exe
2) Legare cai de variabile de sistem(system variables)
    Edit system variables>Path:
        adaugare E:\atmelstudio\7.0\toolchain\avr8\avrassembler
                %USERPROFILE%\AppData\Local\Arduino15\packages\arduino\tools\avrdude\6.3.0-arduino17\bin\ 
                E:\atmelstudio\7.0\toolchain\avr8\avr8-gnu-toolchain\avr\include
    Edit system variables>Add:
                E:\atmelstudio\7.0\toolchain\avr8\avr8-gnu-toolchain\avr\include ;biblioteci
                iom328p.h defineste pinii
    Caile vor fi incluse in functie de unde sunt executabilele instalate de fapt.
